Anuket's Blessing'

After the god of storms and disorder, Seth, cursed a village with a prolonged drought, many of its residents were slowly succumbing to the heat under the scorching sun. Amidst the chaos, a brave villager set out on a journey far from her home in search of the blessings of the goddess Anuket to help restore her society. From Anuket, she received a staff with spells powerful enough to raise water waves out of thin air. With her new power, she starts her journey back to the village, determined to bring justice against Seth and end the drought.
